Courses

Programmes we run.

All courses are faculty-led and mapped to a recognised curriculum or standard. Get in touch to check dates and eligibility for your grade or role.

Postgraduate
CT3 Anaesthesia

ANTS Simulation Course

Eight simulation scenarios for CT3 anaesthetists completing Stage 1 of the RCoA 2021 curriculum — oxygen failure, malignant hyperthermia, major haemorrhage, total power failure and more — each followed by a structured debrief using the ANTS (Anaesthetists' Non-Technical Skills) framework.

Faculty: 1 candidate + plant support 15–20 min sim · 30–45 min debrief per scenario Faculty use only
Faculty development

Clinical Debriefing Course

A course for anyone who runs simulation or supervises trainees: structured models for debriefing (Reactions → Description → Analysis), handling difficult conversations, and giving formative feedback that changes practice.

For simulation faculty & supervisors Practical, small-group format

VR Workshop

Rotating VR and haptic stations for procedural rehearsal and communication scenarios, refined each year with faculty and trainee feedback. A hands-on complement to manikin-based training.

Multi-station workshop format Open to all grades

Emergency Medicine Simulation

In-situ resus and trauma-bay scenarios for Emergency Medicine teams, run on our SimMan 3G manikins with real monitoring equipment, in the department itself where possible.

In-situ or simulation suite Multi-disciplinary teams
